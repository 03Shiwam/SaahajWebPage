Our Story

Saahaj Milk Producer Organization was incorporated on 17th October 2014 with its headquarters located at Agra, Uttar Pradesh. The MPC commenced its business operations from 12th December 2014 with a vision to empower local dairy farmers and deliver premium quality dairy products to consumers.

Our primary objective is to directly procure and process milk from our registered members, ensuring fair compensation and eliminating middlemen. Currently, Saahaj Milk Producer Organization operates across 18 districts of Uttar Pradesh, procuring over 580,000 kg of milk daily from our network of registered members.

We adhere to strict quality control measures and employ modern technology in our processing units to maintain the nutritional integrity of our products. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and farmer welfare has established us as a trusted name in the dairy industry.
